Market Movers Plus is an online platform created for our tabloid newspaper. It allows businesses and individuals to easily book advertisements for our print and digital editions. Our site simplifies the ad booking process with an easy-to-use interface and a range of advertising options.

Booking an ad is simple. Just visit the "Book Ad" section on our website, choose your preferred ad type, size, and edition, fill in the required details, and proceed to payment. Once the booking is confirmed, your ad will be scheduled for publication in the chosen edition.

We offer various ad formats, including classified ads, display ads, banner ads, and sponsored content. You can choose the size, layout, and positioning of your ad based on your budget and preferences. Detailed options are available on our ad booking page.

If you have any questions or need assistance with booking an ad, you can reach out to our customer support team via email at adsupport@marketmoversplus.com, or use the "Contact Us" form on our website. We're here to help make the process as smooth as possible.
